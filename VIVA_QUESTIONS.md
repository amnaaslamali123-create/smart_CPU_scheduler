# Viva Questions and Answers

## Basic Questions

1. What is CPU scheduling?
   Answer: CPU scheduling is the process of deciding which process gets the CPU at a given time.

2. What is a process?
   Answer: A process is a program in execution.

3. What is burst time?
   Answer: Burst time is the total time required by a process to complete execution.

4. What is waiting time?
   Answer: Waiting time is the total time a process spends waiting before getting CPU time.

5. What is turnaround time?
   Answer: Turnaround time is the total time from arrival to completion.

## Algorithm Questions

6. What is FCFS?
   Answer: FCFS means First Come First Serve.

7. What is SJF?
   Answer: SJF means Shortest Job First.

8. What is SRTF?
   Answer: SRTF means Shortest Remaining Time First and is preemptive.

9. What is Round Robin?
   Answer: Round Robin allocates a fixed quantum to each process in cyclic order.

10. Why is Round Robin popular?
    Answer: It is fair and suitable for time-sharing systems.
